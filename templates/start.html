  • Welcome to Vimes

    • Vimes is a simple to do list creator. It was created out of a desire to have a very simple way to create and manage lists. Many other list managers simply offered too much complexity and sophistication than I needed for my purposes. So this was created to have something simple and elegant.
    • All the lists on this page are editable to feel free to test out all the features or sign up and start creating your lists.
    • Vimes uses jQuery, Python, and Flask to work its magic.

  • How to use

    1. Click on any item or header to edit
    2. When editing press enter to create a new item
    3. Use the up and down arrows to navigate the list
    4. Click an item bullet to cross out an item
    5. Double click an item to delete
    6. Click and drag an item bullet to reorder
    7. Click and drag a list header to rearrange lists
    8. Delete all text to remove an item (Press Ctrl+A and then Delete)
