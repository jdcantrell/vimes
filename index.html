  • How to use

    1. Click on any item or header to edit
    2. When editing press enter to create a new item
    3. Use the up and down arrows to navigate the list
    4. Click an item bullet to mark item
    5. Click and drag an item bullet to reorder
    6. Click and drag a list header to rearrange lists
    7. Delete all text to remove an item (Press Ctrl+A and then Delete)
